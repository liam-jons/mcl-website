About MCL

Mason Consulting Limited (MCL) is a pioneering British not-for-profit organisation focused on providing exceptional IT services with a unique emphasis on neurodiversity. With a heritage dating back to 1985, MCL has evolved to offer:

  • Consulting & Strategic Guidance
  • Software Development & Testing
  • AI Readiness & Data Engineering
  • IT Security
  • IT Recruitment Strategy

At least 50% of our technical staff are neurodivergent. We partner with our in-house charity arm, Propel, to ensure our staff and clients receive expert training and support around neurodiversity and inclusion.

MCL's Consultants

Our consultants are moulded by the “Art of Consultancy”—the human element that becomes key as Artificial Intelligence pervades our existence.

Key Characteristics

  • Initiative: They identify opportunities for improvement and act.
  • Integrity: They are honest, transparent, and accountable.
  • Resilience: They persevere in the face of challenges.
  • Communication: They articulate visions clearly and provide feedback.
  • Vision: They set clear goals and inspire others.

Impact on the Environment

  • Inspire Others: They motivate teams to strive for excellence.
  • Catalysts for Change: They challenge the status quo and encourage innovation.
  • Build Relationships: They foster collaboration and mutual respect.
  • Create a Positive Culture: They ensure individuals feel valued and supported.
  • Achieve Results: They set goals, track progress, and celebrate successes.

MCL is people-centric, focused on creating an environment that people WANT to be a part of, as they are truly inspired and can excel.

Our Services

We offer consulting, strategic guidance, development, AI readiness, production support, and more. Our services include:

  • Consulting & Strategic Guidance (IT strategies, AI, project management, digital transformation)
  • Software Development & Testing (innovative solutions tailored to your needs)
  • AI & Data (readiness, engineering, analysis)
  • IT Security (secure systems, risk assessments, compliance)
  • IT Recruitment Strategy (DEI-focused, leveraging neurodivergent talent)

Through our partnership with Propel, we provide neurodiversity training and support, ensuring that both our teams and our clients thrive in an inclusive environment.
